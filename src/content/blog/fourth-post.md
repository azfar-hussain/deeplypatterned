$$
F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} \\
\\
\phi = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{F_n}{F_{n-1}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{F_{n-1}}{F_{n-2}} \\
\\
\phi = 1 + \frac{1}{\phi} \\
\\
\phi \approx 1.61803398875
$$
